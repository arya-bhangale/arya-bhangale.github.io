Gyroscope Assembly

A multi-part unique gyroscope assembly made using Siemens NX, and using interference checks and DFM.

Overview

  • Tools: Siemens NX
  • Focus: Assemblies, constraints, exploded views
  • Deliverables: CAD models, renders, drawings

Design Process

The project began with identifying different gyroscope models and then figuring out which one we wanted to create. Individual components were modeled and then assembled using mating constraints to ensure proper alignment and motion.
